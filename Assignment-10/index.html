Artificial Intelligence:

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence by machines, especially computer systems, to perform tasks that typically require human cognition, such as learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, and language understanding . AI enables machines to analyze data, recognize patterns, make decisions, and even adapt to new information in ways that can mimic human thinking.

Metaverse:

The Metaverse is a virtual, immersive digital universe where users interact with each other and their surroundings through augmented reality (AR), virtual reality (VR), and advanced internet technologies. It integrates physical and digital worlds, allowing people to work, play, socialize, and shop in a 3D environment. The metaverse has the potential to revolutionize industries like gaming, education, entertainment, and even business collaboration, offering new levels of interactivity and experience. With platforms like Meta and Roblox spearheading its development, the metaverse is seen as the next evolution of the internet, often referred to as "Web 3.0." However, its full realization is still in progress, with challenges around privacy, accessibility, and economic models remaining key areas of focus.
